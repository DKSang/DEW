# KPI Definition Rubric

A KPI is well-defined when it has:

## Required

- KPI ID
- KPI name
- business question
- decision supported
- consumer
- formula
- grain
- dimensions
- required fields
- candidate source dependency
- feasibility status

## Strong KPI Definition

A strong KPI definition answers:

1. What does this metric measure?
2. Who uses it?
3. What decision does it support?
4. What is the formula?
5. What is the grain?
6. What dimensions are needed?
7. What fields are required?
8. Which source may provide those fields?
9. What assumptions exist?
10. What must be validated later?

## Failure Conditions

A KPI definition fails if:
- formula is missing
- grain is missing
- required fields are missing
- source dependency is unknown
- it is marked verified without evidence
- it does not map to a business decision
