# Business Discovery Checklist

- [ ] Project brief loaded or context provided.
- [ ] At least one data consumer identified.
- [ ] Core business decision identified.
- [ ] Business questions mapped to consumers.
- [ ] Decision-to-action flow described.
- [ ] Trust expectation previewed.
- [ ] Unknowns labeled.
- [ ] Assumptions labeled.
- [ ] Decision log updated.
- [ ] Learning log updated if learning mode is enabled.
