# Reuse Decision Rubric

Before reusing a pattern, ask:

1. Is the business problem similar?
2. Are source types similar?
3. Is trust expectation similar?
4. Is grain/freshness similar?
5. Is data volume similar?
6. Is team skill level similar?
7. Is operational mode similar?
8. Are caveats acceptable?

If most answers are no, do not reuse blindly.
