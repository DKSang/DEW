# Step 04 — Story Readiness Gate

## Mandatory Rules

- Stop at this gate.
- Story cannot be ready-for-dev without evidence requirements.
- Story cannot be ready-for-dev without testable acceptance criteria.
- User must approve or revise story readiness.

## Readiness options

A. Mark story ready-for-dev  
B. Ready with caveats  
C. Revise story  
D. Block story  
E. Select different story

| Area | Status | Notes |

## User decision

Ask user to choose A/B/C/D/E.

Do not continue until user chooses.
