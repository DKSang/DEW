# Source Candidate Rubric

A candidate source is worth assessing when it has at least:

- source name
- source type
- access method hypothesis
- expected business role
- related KPI or product requirement
- expected grain or unknown grain
- expected freshness or unknown freshness

## Candidate source status

### Candidate

Use when the source is identified but not validated.

### Needs discovery

Use when source exists conceptually but access/provider/details are unclear.

### Duplicate

Use when the source overlaps another source and may not be needed.

### Out of scope

Use when source may be useful but not for MVP.

### Reject early

Use when the source is clearly irrelevant, inaccessible, or unsafe.
